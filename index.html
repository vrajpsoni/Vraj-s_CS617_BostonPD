Nighttime Crime Patterns in Boston

For this data story, I focused on crime reports that happened during nighttime hours in Boston. I defined nighttime as 6 PM through 2 AM so I could compare when incidents are most common and where those incidents are concentrated across the city. Together, these two charts show that nighttime crime is not spread evenly across time or place.

Crimes by Hour

The first chart gives an overview of how many nighttime incidents were reported at each hour. Midnight stands out as the busiest hour in this filtered dataset, while the counts generally decline after that point.

Crime Hotspots Map

The second chart shifts from time to location. Each marker represents a nighttime incident from the processed dataset created in Step 2. Looking at the full set of points makes it easier to see where incidents cluster and which parts of the city stand out during nighttime hours.
